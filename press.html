    Lost Toys

    Lost Toys, a 3D puzzler for iOS about loss and recovery.

    Lost Toys is our first game. We wanted to keep it simple and utilize the platform we had before us: the iPad. Lost Toys is a tactile experience set in a beatiful but distraught setting.

    Who

    Barking Mouse Studio. Yah, so we named our game studio after our dog, named "Mouse". We're a small studio, just getting started with our first game: Lost Toys.
